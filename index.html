Welcome to Segerstrom Football Boosters

We are a group of dedicated parent volunteers working together to support and enhance the Segerstrom High School football program at all levels. As a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, our mission is to provide resources, fundraising support, and coordination to ensure the team can thrive both on and off the field.

On this site, you'll find everything you need to get involved—whether you’re a parent looking for registration info, a community member interested in volunteering, or someone wanting to stay informed on upcoming events.

Use the navigation menu above to explore more about our organization, learn how to contribute, or reach out to us directly.
